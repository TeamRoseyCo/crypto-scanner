## 📞 FIRST 5 CALLS SCRIPT (Practice This)

**Introduction:**
"Hi [Name], this is Bruno from Elevateo Co. How are you?"

**Hook:**
"I'm reaching out to [industry] businesses in [location] to see if you're
currently dealing with [specific pain point]?"

**Bridge:**
"We specialize in [service], and we've helped companies like [similar company]
achieve [specific result]. Would it make sense to have a brief conversation
about how we might be able to help you?"

**Handle Objection:**
- "Not interested" → "No problem! Can I ask—is it not a priority right now,
  or have you already solved this?"
- "Too busy" → "I completely understand. Can I send you a quick case study
  and follow up next month?"
- "Already have a provider" → "Great! If you don't mind me asking, what's
  working well, and what could be better?"

**Close:**
"Perfect! I'll [send you info / book a call / follow up in X days]. Thanks
for your time, [Name]!"

## 🚨 COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

❌ Waiting for "perfect" conditions before starting
❌ Sending generic copy-paste messages
❌ Not tracking your outreach
❌ Giving up after 10 rejections
❌ Talking about features instead of benefits
❌ Not following up (80% of sales happen after 5+ touchpoints)
❌ Forgetting to ask for referrals

✅ Start NOW with what you have
✅ Personalize every message (even slightly)
✅ Log EVERYTHING
✅ Treat rejection as data
✅ Focus on THEIR problems, not YOUR services
✅ Follow up religiously
✅ Ask every client: "Who else do you know who might benefit?"
