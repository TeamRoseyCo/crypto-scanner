## 📞 DISCOVERY CALL FRAMEWORK (15 mins)

1. **Build Rapport** (2 min)
   - Research them beforehand
   - Reference specific details about their business

2. **Situation Questions** (5 min)
   - Current state
   - Challenges they're facing
   - What they've tried before

3. **Problem Questions** (5 min)
   - Pain points
   - Cost of inaction
   - Impact on their business

4. **Implication** (2 min)
   - What happens if they don't solve this?
   - What's possible if they do?

5. **Next Steps** (1 min)
   - Book demo/proposal call
   - Send case study
   - Schedule follow-up

### Discovery Questions Bank

**Situation:**
- "What's your current process for [relevant area]?"
- "How many people are involved in [process]?"
- "What tools are you currently using?"

**Problems:**
- "What's working well? What's frustrating?"
- "Where do things typically fall apart?"
- "What keeps you up at night about [area]?"

**Impact:**
- "If you could wave a magic wand, what would change?"
- "What's this costing you in time/money/opportunity?"
- "How would solving this impact your business?"

**Timeline:**
- "When do you need this solved by?"
- "What happens if you don't solve this by then?"
- "What's driving the urgency?"
